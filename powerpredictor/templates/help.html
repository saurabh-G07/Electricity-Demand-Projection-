Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the AI Power Demand Prediction project?

The AI Power Demand Prediction project aims to optimize energy consumption in our state using AI algorithms that analyze various factors like weather, population, and historical data to predict future power demands.

2. How can I contribute to the project?

You can contribute by providing financial support, partnering as a public-private initiative, or sharing data relevant to energy consumption and weather patterns.

3. Who can I contact for technical issues?

For any technical issues or queries, please contact our technical support team via the contact form below or email us at support@powerai.in.

4. How often is the data updated?

The data is updated in real-time, utilizing smart meters and weather data to provide accurate and timely predictions. Regular assessments are conducted to refine the model further.

5. How do I report an issue with the system?

If you encounter any issues, please use the contact form below to report your concerns, and our team will address them promptly.
